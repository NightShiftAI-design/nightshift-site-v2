Frequently Asked Questions

      

Everything you need to know about NightShift AI

    
  
     
    
      
        

What is NightShift AI?

        

NightShift AI is a 24/7 hotel voice assistant that answers calls, books reservations, handles questions, and alerts managers instantly via SMS and email.

      
      
        

Does the AI sound human?

        

No robotic voice. NightShift uses natural human pacing, tone, and emotion. Guests routinely assume it’s a real person.

      
      
        

Can it actually book rooms?

        

Yes. It collects arrival dates, guest count, pets, smoking preference, calculates totals, and saves the reservation into your dashboard instantly.

      
      
        

Is it ADA & PCI compliant?

        

Fully. NightShift follows all ADA service animal rules and never accepts credit card numbers over the phone.

      
      
        

How fast does it answer calls?

        

Every call is answered in under 3 seconds — day or night.

      
      
        

What languages does it support?

        

English, Spanish, Hindi, Gujarati, and French — automatically detected and switched.

      
      
        

Does it replace my staff?

        

It can, but most hotels use NightShift to cover nights, overflow calls, and days when the front desk is busy.

      
      
        

Can it handle emergencies?

        

Yes — NightShift immediately instructs callers to dial 911, then offers to connect them to the front desk afterward.

      
      
        

Does it save call logs?

        

Every call is saved with transcript, summary, sentiment score, tags, and reservation outcome inside your dashboard.

      
      
        

How long does setup take?

        

Less than 10 minutes. Most hotels go live the same day.

      
      
        

How does billing work?

        

Simple monthly pricing per property with no contracts. Cancel anytime.
